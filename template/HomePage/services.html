Our Services

Dry Cleaning

Dry washing cleans items without using water, ideal for delicate fabrics, carpets, and upholstery. Methods include:
Dry Cleaning: Uses chemical solvents.
Dry Powder: Sprinkle and vacuum absorbent powder.
Dry Foam: Apply foam, let dry, then vacuum.
Dry Compound: Spread granular cleaner and vacuum.
Spot Cleaning: Dab stains with cleaning solvent.

Laundry

Sorting: Clothes are sorted by color, fabric type, and soil level.
Pre-Treatment: Stains are treated before washing.
Washing: Loaded into machines with proper detergent.
Drying: Based on fabric, tumble or air dried.
Folding & Ironing: Clothes are folded or ironed.
Final Inspection & Packaging: Ensuring quality.
Delivery: Delivered or picked up.

Stain Removal

Identify: Type of stain and fabric.
Inspect: Care labels and stains.
Pre-Treat: Specialized stain removers.
Agitate: Light rubbing.
Rinse: With water.
Launder & Post-Treat: Re-clean if needed.
Dry: As per fabric.

Steam Ironing

Inspect & Sort: By fabric.
Pre-Treat: Spot clean.
Steam Prep: Fill and heat steamer.
Ironing: Smooth strokes.
Finish: Collars, cuffs, etc.
Cool & Inspect: Let set.
Hang or Fold: For delivery.
